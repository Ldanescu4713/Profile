Enterprise Virtualization & Data Centre Consolidation

Senior Server Administrator → IT Coordinator

  • Situation: ~100 aging servers across multiple sites causing downtime and high cost.
  • Task: Modernize and centralize infrastructure while improving reliability.
  • Action: Led migration to clustered hypervisors (VMware); executed P2V; standardized backups; documented SOPs.
  • Result: 2-host cluster (~50 VMs); >90% cost reduction; improved HA and DR via replication.
